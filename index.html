Brief Biography

I am now a Associate Professor at the National Key Laboratory of Space Integrated Information System, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. I received my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof.Changwen Zheng from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2022. From 2022 to 2024, I conducted a two-year postdoctoral fellowship at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Professor Changwen Zheng. I have authored more than 50 papers in international journals and conferences including IJCV, IEEE TKDE, IEEE TGRS, ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, ACM MM, IJCAI, and so on. I serve as a regular reviewer member for a number of journals and conferences, e.g., T-PAMI, IJCV, TIP, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, WWW, and so on. My main research interests lie in artificial intelligence, machine learning, causal inference, and LLM/MLLM.
